GUESS THE NUMBER

EASY LEVEL

This is the Easiest Level of the Guess the Number Game and it is between the numbers 1 and 10 and you have to hit the random number that the machine gives you.

MIDDLE LEVEL

This is the Medium Level that is in the Guess the Number game and this includes the numbers between 1 and 50 and you have to guess the random number that the machine gives you.

HARD LEVEL

This is the Hardest Level of the Guess the Number game and this is between the numbers 1 and 100 and you have to hit the random number that the machine gives you.
